  1. Introduction to AI

    Artificial Intelligence (AI) enables machines to mimic human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making.

    Example: Virtual assistants and recommendation systems.

    Old approach: Rule-based systems Modern approach: Data-driven learning

    Quote: AI is the new electricity.

    AI is not just automation, it enhances human capability.

    Note: AI systems require large amounts of data.

  2. Machine Learning

    Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI where models learn patterns from data.

    Types include: Supervised, Unsupervised, and Reinforcement Learning.

    Formula example: y = f(x)

    Let x represent input data and output as prediction.

    Sample code:

    model.fit(data, labels);

    Output: Training Complete

    Shortcut: Ctrl + Enter

  3. Deep Learning

    Deep Learning uses multi-layer neural networks for complex tasks.

    Used in image recognition, speech processing, and automation.

    Water formula: H2O | Square: x2

    Deep Learning Book is widely used for study.
